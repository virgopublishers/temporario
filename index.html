Quick Notes

This app uses only the Major Arcana that consists of 22 cards. This Tarot reading method can give us a more precise answer to substantial questions of our lives.

When formulating your question, avoid the ones that can only be answered with yes or no. For example, instead of asking, "Will I get married next year", you should ask, "What do I need to do to get married next year."

You should also avoid asking about what'll happen in the future because there isn't a definite answer to this kind of question since it depends a lot on our own choices.
